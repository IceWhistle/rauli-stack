## 1. COGNITIVE OPTIMIZATION STACKS

### 1.1 RACETAM + CHOLINE SYNERGY

**The Core Stack:**
- **Piracetam:** 1,200-4,800 mg/day (split 2-3 doses)
- **Aniracetam:** 750-1,500 mg/day (split 2 doses)
- **Phenylpiracetam:** 100-200 mg/day (cycle 2 weeks on, 2 weeks off)
- **Alpha-GPC or CDP-Choline:** 300-600 mg/day (with each racetam dose)

**Synergy Mechanism:**
- Racetams upregulate acetylcholine receptors and increase receptor density
- This DEPLETES acetylcholine stores → causes "racetam headaches"
- Choline sources replenish acetylcholine, creating synergistic cognitive boost
- Studies show **combined effects 3-5x greater** than either compound alone

**Optimal Ratio:** 4:1 piracetam to aniracetam when stacking multiple racetams

**Timing:**
- Morning dose on empty stomach
- Second dose early afternoon (avoid evening if sleep disrupted)
- Take choline WITH each racetam dose
- Effects build over 4-6 weeks to maximum

**Cycling:** 
- Piracetam/Aniracetam: Can run continuously, but 8-12 week cycles recommended
- Phenylpiracetam: MUST cycle 2 weeks on/2 weeks off to prevent tolerance

**⚠️ CAUTION:**

## 3. LONGEVITY COMPOUND SYNERGIES

### 3.1 NAD+ OPTIMIZATION STACK

**The Core Protocol:**
- **NMN:** 500-1,000 mg/morning (sublingual preferred)
- **Resveratrol:** 500-1,000 mg/morning (with fats)

- NMN → NAD+ precursor (direct pathway)
- Resveratrol: SIRT1 activator (NAD+-dependent enzyme)
- Pterostilbene: More bioavailable resveratrol analog, SIRT1 activation
- TMG: Methyl donor (prevents methylation drain from NMN)

**Clinical Evidence:**
- NMN + Resveratrol: 1.59x heart NAD+, 1.72x muscle NAD+ vs NMN alone
- NMN + Ginsenosides: 1.97x lung NAD+ vs NMN alone
- Pterostilbene + NR (similar to NMN): Sustained NAD+ elevation in humans

**Timing:**
- **Morning, fasted:** Best aligns with circadian NAD+ rhythms
- Take resveratrol/pterostilbene WITH healthy fats (olive oil, avocado, fish oil)
- NMN sublingually for faster absorption
- TMG with or after NMN

**Cycling:**
- Can run continuously
- Some users cycle 5 days on, 2 days off
- No tolerance issues reported

**David Sinclair Protocol (Reference):**
- NMN: 1g/morning
- Resveratrol: 1g/morning (with yogurt)
- Metformin: 800 mg/day (prescription)

### 3.2 SENOLYTIC PROTOCOLS

**Primary Protocol (Dasatinib + Quercetin):**
- **Dasatinib:** 100 mg/day for 2 consecutive days
- **Quercetin:** 1,000-2,000 mg/day for 2 consecutive days
- **Frequency:** Once every 2-4 weeks initially, then monthly maintenance

**Alternative Protocol (Fisetin):**
- **Fisetin:** 100-500 mg/day for 2 consecutive days
- **Frequency:** Monthly or quarterly
- Often combined with D+Q for synergy

**Synergy Mechanism:**
- Dasatinib: Inhibits Src tyrosine kinase (kills senescent cells via apoptosis)
- Quercetin: Inhibits Bcl-xL and other anti-apoptotic proteins
- Fisetin: Senolytic via multiple pathways, also flavonoid with antioxidant properties
- **Different senescent cell types respond to different senolytics**

**Timing Protocol:**
- Take on an empty stomach in morning
- 2 days in a row (Mon-Tue or Sat-Sun)
- Then abstain until next cycle
- Track biomarkers (inflammatory markers, perceived benefits)

**Cycling Rationale:**
- Senolytics are "hit and run" - eliminate senescent cells, then clear system
- Continuous use NOT beneficial and may have side effects
- Intermittent dosing allows body to clear debris and regenerate

**Clinical Evidence:**
- D+Q: First human evidence of senescent cell reduction (2019)
- Fisetin: Increased mouse lifespan ~20%, reduced senescent cell markers
- D+Q+F combo studied in 2024 - showed epigenetic age improvements

**⚠️ CAUTION:**
- Dasatinib is prescription chemotherapy drug (obtain legally)
- Can cause thrombocytopenia (low platelets) - avoid if on blood thinners
- Quercetin can interfere with certain medications
- Start with lower doses, assess tolerance

## 4. HORMONE OPTIMIZATION

### 4.1 FULL TRT PROTOCOL

**Base Protocol:**
- **Testosterone Enanthate or Cypionate:** 100-200 mg/week (split 2-3 injections)

- **Microdosing approach:** 17.5 mg testosterone daily (subcutaneous)
  - More stable levels, less aromatization, better feeling

- **HCG:** Maintain testicular function, fertility, backfill hormonal pathways

**AI Guidelines:**
- **Only if high E2 symptoms:** Gyno, water retention, emotional, low libido

- **DHEA:** 25-50 mg/day (if low on labs)

**Monitoring:**
- Pre-TRT: Total T, Free T, E2 (sensitive), LH, FSH, SHBG, CBC, CMP

- Quarterly: Full panel + lipids, PSA (if 40+)

**⚠️ TRT IS LIFELONG:**
- Natural production shuts down within weeks

## 6. SUPPLEMENT TIMING & ABSORPTION

### 6.1 FAT-SOLUBLE VS WATER-SOLUBLE

**Fat-Soluble (Take with fats):**
- **Vitamins A, D, E, K** - morning with breakfast
- **CoQ10, PQQ** - morning/afternoon with fats
- **Resveratrol, Pterostilbene** - morning with fats
- **Curcumin** - with black pepper + fats
- **Omega-3 fish oil** - with any meal

**Water-Soluble (Empty stomach OK):**
- **Vitamin C** - morning, can split doses
- **B Vitamins** - morning (can be energizing)
- **Peptides (BPC, TB, etc)** - fasted, away from meals
- **Amino acids (Glycine, Taurine, Tyrosine)** - fasted or between meals

---

### 6.2 MINERAL TIMING (Critical for Absorption)

**DO NOT COMBINE:**
- **Calcium + Iron** → Separate by 2+ hours
- **Zinc + Copper** → Take separately (zinc blocks copper)
- **Zinc + Iron** → Separate by 30+ minutes
- **Calcium + Magnesium + Zinc (CMZ formulas)** → Avoid taking near standalone iron

**Optimal Timing:**
- **Calcium:** Morning or split AM/PM (max 500mg per dose for absorption)
- **Magnesium:** Evening (promotes sleep, muscle relaxation)
- **Zinc:** Evening with food (away from iron/calcium/copper)
- **Iron:** Morning, fasted, with Vitamin C (enhances absorption)
- **Copper:** 2-3 mg/day if taking zinc long-term (prevent deficiency)

---

### 6.3 CYCLING PROTOCOLS

**Compounds That REQUIRE Cycling:**

1. **Phenylpiracetam:** 2 weeks on, 2 weeks off (rapid tolerance)
2. **Fadogia Agrestis:** 8 weeks on, 4 weeks off (testicular safety)
3. **SARMs:** 8-10 weeks on, 4-8 weeks off + PCT
4. **Peptides (GH secretagogues):** 5 days on, 2 days off OR 8-12 weeks on, 4-8 weeks off
5. **Senolytics:** 2 days on, 2-4 weeks off (not meant for continuous use)

**Compounds That BENEFIT from Cycling:**

1. **Ashwagandha:** 8 weeks on, 2 weeks off (though some use continuously)
2. **Rhodiola:** 8-12 weeks on, 2-4 weeks off
3. **Racetams:** 8-12 weeks on, 4 weeks off (tolerance prevention)
4. **Caffeine:** Periodic 1-2 week breaks restore sensitivity

**Compounds That DON'T Need Cycling:**

1. **Creatine:** Continuous use fine (maintenance 3-5g/day)
2. **Omega-3:** Continuous (building cell membranes)
3. **Magnesium, Vitamin D, Zinc:** Daily use essential
4. **CoQ10, PQQ:** Continuous for mitochondrial support
5. **Choline sources:** Daily use fine when stacking with racetams

---

### 6.4 WHAT NOT TO COMBINE

**Dangerous/Counterproductive Combinations:**

1. **Multiple SARMs + Prohormones + AAS** → Excessive hepatoxicity, HPTA shutdown
2. **Multiple GH secretagogues at max doses** → Desensitization, excessive IGF-1
3. **High-dose iodine + Selenium deficiency** → Can trigger autoimmune thyroiditis
4. **MAOIs + Tyrosine/Mucuna Pruriens** → Hypertensive crisis
5. **Blood thinners + High-dose Vitamin E/K** → Bleeding risk or clotting issues
6. **Multiple stimulating racetams** → Overstimulation, anxiety, insomnia
7. **Dihexa + NSI-189 (initial use)** → Overwhelming neurogenesis, brain fog
8. **Calcium + Iron (together)** → Blocks iron absorption

**Absorption Interference:**

1. **Zinc inhibits copper** → Always supplement copper if taking zinc long-term
2. **Calcium blocks iron** → Separate by 2+ hours
3. **Fiber/Phytates + Minerals** → Reduce absorption, take minerals away from high-fiber meals
4. **Green tea/Coffee + Iron** → Tannins block absorption
5. **Antibiotics + Minerals** → Most need 2+ hour separation
