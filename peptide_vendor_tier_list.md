## ⚠️ RED FLAGS TO AVOID

1. **No COA/Testing:** Amino Asylum (peptides), random Alibaba sellers
2. **Too Cheap:** <$3/mg for BPC-157 suggests fake/underdosed
3. **No Physical Address:** Legit companies list real locations
4. **Only Crypto:** Risk of exit scams (though many legit vendors accept crypto)
5. **"Human Grade" Claims:** Research peptides are NOT for human consumption
