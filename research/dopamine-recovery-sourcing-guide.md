## 📋 PROTOCOL GUIDANCE

### 9-Me-BC Cycle Length (Based on User Reports)

**Common Protocols:**
- **Short Cycle:** 2-4 weeks on, 2-4 weeks off
- **Medium Cycle:** 4-6 weeks on, 4 weeks off
- **Microdosing:** 5-10mg daily for extended periods (8-12 weeks)

**Typical Dosage:**
- 10-20mg daily (oral)
- Some users report benefits at 5mg
- **CRITICAL:** Avoid sunlight exposure during use (photosensitivity)

### Stacking Warnings

**⚠️ DO NOT STACK WITH:**
- Other stimulants (caffeine okay in moderation)
- MAO inhibitors
- SSRIs (consult doctor)
- Other dopaminergic drugs without medical supervision

**✅ SAFE TO STACK:**
- Uridine + CDP-Choline + DHA (Mr. Happy Stack base)
- Bromantane (complementary mechanism)
- NA-Semax (different pathway)
- Mucuna (natural L-DOPA - use cautiously)

### Mr. Happy Stack Protocol
- Uridine Monophosphate: 150-300mg daily
- CDP-Choline: 250-500mg daily
- DHA: 1000mg+ daily
- **Timing:** Take with meals (especially DHA)
