## 🧬 DSIP PROTOCOLS (Delta Sleep-Inducing Peptide)

### What It Is
Nonapeptide that induces delta EEG activity and spindle waves. First isolated from rabbit brains.

### Real User Protocols
| Protocol | Dose | Frequency | Notes |
|----------|------|-----------|-------|
| Conservative | 50mcg | 1x/week | Start here; assess tolerance |
| Moderate | 100-200mcg | 2-3x/week | Most common protocol |
| Aggressive | 25nmol/kg IV | Research only | Clinical studies only |

### Administration
- **Route:** Subcutaneous injection (most common)
- **Timing:** 30-60 minutes before bed
- **Bioavailability:** IV > SubQ > Oral (oral largely ineffective)

### User Experiences
- "4 hours of REM vs 0.5-1.5 hours normally when training hard"
- "Inception-level lucid dreams - almost too vivid"
- "Best used as a tool, not daily - weekends only to reset circadian rhythm"
- "Night 3-5 is when the real pattern shows up"

### Warnings
- Can cause daytime grogginess if dosed too high
- Some users report it "doesn't click" for them at all
- Not for daily continuous use

## 🌡️ TEMPERATURE MANIPULATION

### 8Sleep / Eight Sleep Pod
- **Temp Range:** 55°F - 110°F
- **User Results:** 10% average increase in deep sleep (per 8Sleep study with Oura)
- **Protocol:** Cooler temps for deep sleep (early night), slightly warmer for REM (later)
- **Cost:** ~$2,000+ for system + $15-20/month subscription
- **Verdict:** "Game changer if you have the money" / "Not worth it if your room temp is already controlled"

### Cold Exposure Timing
| Timing | Protocol | Effect |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Morning | 2-3 min cold shower/plunge | Increases alertness, sets circadian rhythm |
| Evening (early) | 5-10 min ice bath | Lowers core temp, promotes sleep onset |
| Late evening | Avoid | Can spike cortisol, delay sleep |

### User Experiences
- "Cold plunge 2 hours before bed = higher HRV, lower resting HR during sleep"
- "Every extended cold session shows HRV increase and RHR decrease on WHOOP"
- "3 days of cold + sauna cycling showed significant HRV improvements"

### Cold Plunge Protocol
- **Duration:** Start 1-2 min, work up to 3-5 min
- **Temperature:** ≤15°C (59°F)
- **Frequency:** 3-5x/week for sleep benefits

## ☀️ LIGHT EXPOSURE PROTOCOLS

### Morning Sun (CRITICAL)
| Window | Duration | Why |
|--------|----------|-----|
| Within 30 min of waking | 2-10 min | Sets circadian clock, starts melatonin timer |

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|

| Bedroom | Use red/amber lights only |

- **Sky Lights:** Get morning sun through skylight if possible
- **SAD Lamp:** 10,000 lux for 20-30 min on dark mornings
- **Blue Blockers:** Put on 2-3 hours before target bedtime

## 🌿 APIGENIN, L-THEANINE & GABAERGICS

### Apigenin
- **Dose:** 50mg
- **Source:** Chamomile extract
- **Effect:** Binds benzodiazepine receptors (GABA-A), promotes relaxation
- **Stack:** Core component of Huberman stack

### L-Theanine
- **Dose:** 100-400mg
- **Effect:** Alpha brain wave promotion, GABA/glutamate modulation
- **Warning:** Can cause vivid dreams; some users wake up groggy
- **Best use:** With afternoon caffeine to smooth out the crash, or in sleep stack

### GABA (Gamma-Aminobutyric Acid)
- **Dose:** 100-300mg (some use up to 500-1000mg)
- **Timing:** 30-60 min before bed
- **Form:** PharmaGABA (fermented) preferred over synthetic
- **Effect:** Reduces sleep latency ~5 minutes in studies
- **User note:** "100mg works brilliantly; take 1-2 before bed, another if I wake up"

### L-Tryptophan vs 5-HTP
| | L-Tryptophan | 5-HTP |
|-----|--------------|-------|
| Dose | 1,000-3,000mg | 50-200mg |
| Pathway | Converts to 5-HTP → Serotonin → Melatonin | Direct to serotonin → melatonin |
| Safety | Safer long-term | Don't combine with SSRIs |
| Recommendation | Preferred for regular use | Occasional use, more potent |

**Note:** Don't stack 5-HTP + L-Tryptophan together (redundant)
