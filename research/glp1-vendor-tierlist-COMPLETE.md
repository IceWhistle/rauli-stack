## 🎓 FORUM WISDOM

### Key Learnings:

1. **"You WILL eventually get burned"**
   - Never spend more per order than you can afford to lose
   - Spread orders across vendors
   - Start small with new vendors

2. **"Pick 2 of 3: Price, Quality, Customer Service"**
   - Cheap + Quality = Poor service (old QSC)
   - Cheap + Service = Questionable quality
   - Quality + Service = Expensive (Skye, Aminos)

3. **US Warehouses Are Everywhere Now**
   - HPT, MPS, GYC, ERP, APS (coming), SRY
   - Domestic speed without domestic markup (mostly)
   - Game-changer for 2025-2026 market

4. **Testing Is Critical**
   - Always verify COAs on Janoshik website
   - Consider 3rd party testing for large orders
   - Join forum testing groups
   - Filter before use (PeptideTest videos recommended)

5. **Discord Drama**
   - Many vendor Discords getting banned/nuked
   - Always save backup email contacts
   - Telegram more stable than Discord currently
