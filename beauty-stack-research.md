## 🚫 WHAT DOESN'T WORK (COPE INGREDIENTS)

### ❌ **Biotin alone for hair growth**
- Only helps if deficient (rare)
- Most people aren't deficient
- Won't grow hair unless you have a deficiency

### ❌ **Generic collagen without peptides**
- Must be hydrolyzed peptides to be bioavailable
- Gelatin ≠ collagen peptides

### ❌ **Low-dose Vitamin C (<10%)**
- Too weak to penetrate or stimulate collagen
- Needs 10-20% L-ascorbic acid

### ❌ **"Hair growth shampoos"**
- Can't work in 30 seconds on scalp
- Marketing scam
- Use rosemary oil instead (30min massage)

### ❌ **Hyaluronic acid without humectants**
- Can dehydrate skin in dry climates
- Needs occlusive layer on top
