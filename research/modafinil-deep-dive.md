## MECHANISM OF ACTION

### Primary Mechanisms:
1. **Dopamine Reuptake Inhibition** - Blocks DAT transporter, keeps dopamine active longer (similar to methylphenidate/Ritalin but weaker)
2. **Norepinephrine Enhancement** - Boosts NE in the prefrontal cortex → increased alertness, vigilance
3. **Histamine Increase** - Activates histaminergic neurons in hypothalamus → wakefulness
4. **Orexin/Hypocretin Activation** - Stimulates wake-promoting orexin neurons (why it works for narcolepsy)
5. **Glutamate Increase** - Enhances excitatory transmission → learning/memory
6. **GABA Reduction** - Decreases inhibitory GABA in cortex → increased neural activity

### Why It Feels "Cleaner" Than Amphetamines:
- Much weaker dopamine effect (doesn't cause euphoria/crash)
- Doesn't significantly affect serotonin
- Minimal peripheral sympathetic activation
- No significant norepinephrine release (just reuptake inhibition)

## MODAFINIL vs ARMODAFINIL

| Factor | Modafinil (Provigil) | Armodafinil (Nuvigil) |
|--------|---------------------|----------------------|
| Composition | R + S enantiomers (50/50) | R-enantiomer only |
| Half-life | 12-15 hours | 15-18 hours |
| Peak plasma | 2-4 hours | 2 hours (then sustained) |
| Duration | 10-12 hours effective | 12-15 hours effective |
| Feeling | Slightly more "pushy" | Smoother, more sustained |
| Standard dose | 100-200mg | 75-150mg |
| Cost | Cheaper generics | Slightly more expensive |

**Recommendation:** Try both. Many prefer armodafinil for the smoother curve. Start with modafinil since it's cheaper.

## DOSING PROTOCOLS

### Standard Dosing:
- **Starting dose:** 50-100mg (half a standard pill)
- **Typical dose:** 100-200mg
- **Max dose:** 400mg (rarely needed, more side effects)
- **Timing:** Early morning, 6-8am (never after noon due to half-life)

### Optimized Protocol:
1. **Week 1-2:** 50mg to assess tolerance
2. **Week 3+:** 100mg if needed
3. **Only if necessary:** 200mg for heavy cognitive demands
4. **Frequency:** 2-4x per week max (avoid daily use)

### Cycling:
- Use 3-4 days on, 3-4 days off
- Take weekends off
- Consider 2 weeks on, 1 week off for heavy users
- This prevents tolerance and maintains effectiveness

## GENETIC FACTORS (Important!)

**COMT Gene (Rs4680 - Val158Met):**
- **Val/Val (G/G):** Modafinil works BEST - significant cognitive enhancement
- **Val/Met (A/G):** Moderate response
- **Met/Met (A/A):** May get LESS benefit - already have higher baseline dopamine

If you have 23andMe data, check this SNP. ~25% of people are Met/Met and may not respond as well.

## SIDE EFFECTS & MITIGATION

### Common (>10%):
| Side Effect | Cause | Mitigation |
|-------------|-------|------------|
| Headache | Dehydration, vasoconstriction | Drink 2-3L water, add electrolytes |
| Appetite loss | Dopamine/histamine effects | Set meal reminders, eat anyway |
| Insomnia | Long half-life | Take early AM only, never after 10am |
| Anxiety | Stimulation in sensitive individuals | Add L-Theanine 200mg, lower dose |
| Dry mouth | Histamine effects | Hydration |

### Uncommon (1-10%):
- Nausea - Take with food
- Dizziness - Lower dose
- Diarrhea - Usually temporary
- Irritability - May need lower dose

### Rare but Serious:
- **Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS)** - STOP IMMEDIATELY if you get any skin rash, blisters, or mouth sores. This is a medical emergency. Risk ~1 in 100,000.
- **Psychiatric effects** - Rare mania/psychosis in predisposed individuals
