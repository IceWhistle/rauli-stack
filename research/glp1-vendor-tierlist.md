## ADDITIONAL NOTES

### Forum Wisdom
- **"You will eventually get burned"** - Common advice is to never spend more per order than you're willing to lose
- **Spread orders across vendors** - Don't put all eggs in one basket
- **Join Telegram groups** - Best way to get updates on stock, deals, and issues
- **Test your products** - Consider 3rd party testing for large orders
- **Filter before use** - Many recommend filtering peptides before injection

### Testing Resources
- **Janoshik** - Primary testing lab mentioned repeatedly
- **PeptideTest** - Leading educator on filtering/sterility
- **Forum testing groups** - 3rd party community testing available

### Price vs Quality Triangle
The forum consistently mentions: **"Pick 2 of 3: Price, Quality, Customer Service"**
- Cheap + Quality = Poor service (QSC historically)
- Cheap + Service = Questionable quality (various)
- Quality + Service = Expensive (Skye, Aminos)
