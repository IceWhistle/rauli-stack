Thin evidence. Studies are tiny. CoQ10 covers mitochondria better.
CDP-Choline (300mg)
Redundant — Alpha-GPC already covers choline needs.
Liposomal Curcumin (500mg)
Anti-inflammatory is redundant with Omega-3 at 4g. Bioavailability still questionable.
Spermidine (10mg)
Autophagy data mostly from food sources. Supplement evidence too thin for the price.
Fisetin (daily 200mg)
Senolytics work as pulse, not daily. Moved to monthly protocol where it belongs.
Noopept, Dihexa
9-Me-BC + Bromantane + Semax already cover cognitive/dopamine. Dihexa is too experimental at scale. Noopept is redundant with this stack.
SS-31, Humanin, NSI-189, P21, ISRIB, 5-amino-1MQ
Cutting edge ≠ ready. Insufficient human data. Revisit in 2027.
Taurine (morning dose)
GABAergic = nighttime compound. Moved entirely to sleep stack.
Tongkat Ali (400mg)
Boosts LH → testosterone. On TRT you bypass LH entirely. Dead weight.
Ashwagandha KSM-66 (600mg)
Half its value was testosterone. Cortisol reduction alone doesn't justify the slot with this stack.
Boron (9mg)
Free testosterone boost is its main play. On TRT, free T is managed by dose + SHBG.
